Inspiration

So, now that we know what a calisthenic is, how do we begin? Calisthenics can be intimidating at first, especially if you watch videos on YouTube. Starting with the fundamental movements is the greatest approach to begin into calisthenics. As you gain strength and control over your body, you can adopt harder or cooler activities. This page contains the four core regions of calisthenics, from which all additional exercises stem. These exercises, as well as the films connected to them, can be used to create your own program.

Chris Heria

Chris Heria is an American fitness YouTuber who is possibly the most well-known of all the calisthenics influencers. He is a well-known YouTuber who provides his fans with well-produced videos to help them succeed. He goes over everything from how to get started to how to progress and what foods to eat to achieve the greatest results. Follow him on social media and get some inspiration from his thousands of YouTube videos if you haven't already.

Browney

Browney is a popular Dutch YouTuber with over 2 million followers. Unlike Chris,Browney is more interested in the fun and playfull side of chalistenics. He has a lot of challenges and transformation videos on his channel. With his 90-day transformation program, he has lately begun to assist others.

Hannibal for King

Hannibal For King is a calisthenics legend. With his YouTube teaching videos, he continues to inspire millions of people all around the world. He is widely regarded as one of the best bodyweight calisthenics practitioners living. Some of the workouts are quite intense. He grew up in a rough area, and his training helped him keep out of trouble. That's how it all began. It's been more than 20 years since I started doing calisthenics.
